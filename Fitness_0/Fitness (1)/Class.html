Classes Schedule

Monday

  • 9:00 AM - Yoga Flow (60 mins) - Beginner-friendly class focusing on improving flexibility, strength, and relaxation through flowing sequences and breath work.
  • 6:00 PM - HIIT Circuit (45 mins) - High-intensity interval training combining cardio and strength exercises for a full-body workout.

Tuesday

  • 7:30 AM - Core Blast (45 mins) - Targeted core workout to strengthen and tone your abs, obliques, and lower back muscles.
  • 6:30 PM - Spin Class (45 mins) - Indoor cycling class designed to improve cardiovascular endurance and lower body strength.

Wednesday

  • 10:00 AM - Functional Fitness (60 mins) - Focuses on functional movements to improve everyday activities and overall fitness levels.
  • 7:00 PM - Zumba Dance Party (60 mins) - Fun and energetic dance workout set to Latin-inspired music for a full-body cardio workout.

Thursday

  • 6:30 AM - Sunrise Bootcamp (45 mins) - Outdoor bootcamp-style workout combining strength, cardio, and agility drills to kickstart your day.
  • 6:00 PM - Pilates Fusion (60 mins) - Blend of Pilates and strength training exercises to improve posture, flexibility, and core strength.

Friday

  • 12:00 PM - Lunchtime Express (30 mins) - Quick and effective workout for those short on time, focusing on high-intensity exercises to maximize results.
  • 5:30 PM - Strength Training 101 (45 mins) - Introduction to basic strength training exercises and techniques to build muscle and increase strength.

Saturday

  • 9:00 AM - Total Body Conditioning (60 mins) - Full-body workout incorporating strength, cardio, and flexibility exercises for a comprehensive fitness experience.
  • 11:00 AM - Yoga Sculpt (75 mins) - Dynamic yoga practice combined with strength training exercises using light weights to sculpt and tone muscles.

Sunday

  • 10:00 AM - Family Fitness (45 mins) - Fun and interactive workout for parents and kids to enjoy together, focusing on movement, coordination, and bonding.

How to Sign Up:

To sign up for classes, please visit our website or contact our front desk staff. Members can also use our mobile app to reserve their spot in advance.

Drop-In Options:

Drop-in options are available for most classes, depending on availability. Please check our schedule and contact us for more information on drop-in rates.
